Example day on this plan

Here’s how those numbers could look as normal meals you’d actually eat. No boiled-chicken misery.

Breakfast · 20–25% of your calories
—

Greek yogurt bowl with berries, honey, and granola. + 1–2 scrambled eggs or egg whites on the side.

Lunch · 25–30% of your calories
—

Bowl with grilled chicken, rice or potatoes, mixed veggies, and a simple sauce (olive oil, tahini, or yogurt-based).

Dinner · 25–30% of your calories
—

Lean meat or fish, carbs you like (pasta, rice, pita, tortillas), and a decent portion of vegetables or salad.

Snacks · 15–20% of your calories
—

Mix of protein snacks (protein shake, cottage cheese, deli meat), fruit, and fun foods you actually enjoy in moderation.
