Example day of eating

  • Breakfast: Greek yogurt bowl with berries, granola, and honey.
  • Snack 1: Banana + handful of nuts.
  • Lunch: Rice, chicken or beans, veggies, and a simple sauce.
  • Snack 2 (pre-training): Toast with peanut butter + fruit.
  • Dinner: Protein (chicken, beef, tofu, fish) + potatoes or rice + veggies.

In the future, NutriMind will swap each of these for options that match your culture, budget, and what foods you actually like.

Simple training structure

  • 3–4 days of focused training (track / lifting / sport).
  • 1–2 lighter days: walking, mobility, recovery work.
  • 1 full rest day with emphasis on sleep and hydration.

Your AI coach will later adjust volume and intensity based on how you report soreness, sleep, and schedule each week.

Non-negotiables for progress

  • Hit protein most days — not perfection, just consistency.
  • Keep 1–2 “easy” go-to meals you can make half asleep.
  • Drink water throughout the day, not all at night.
  • Sleep at least 7 hours when you can — this moves the needle.

What NutriMind AI will add

  • Adjust calories automatically based on check-ins and weight trend.
  • Change carbs around heavy training days versus off days.
  • Suggest grocery lists that match your budget and store.
  • Answer questions like “What should I eat before my 400m race?”
